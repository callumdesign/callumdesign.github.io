This project was completed in cooperation with Micro Mobility and Victoria University of Wellington. The service aims to provide Micro customers with an appealing and convenient method of charging electric scooters in order to encourage their widespread use.

Micro Charge on the Go builds on Micro Mobility's "world first" branding value to reinforce the idea that electric scooters are a useful and convenient urban transport option.

A smart phone app reports the charging status, as well as allowing access to a locking mechanism to ensure the scooter is kept safe when left for a period of time.
