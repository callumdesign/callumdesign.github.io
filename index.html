About me

My experience in the field of design

I am a recent graduate with a bachelor of design innovation degree, majoring in industrial design. My strengths include CAD and CAM, and design for additive manufacturing. I am experienced in using Solidworks and Rhino to generate prototypes, and I can produce some stunning lifelike renders using Keyshot.

I am familiar with a plethora of design methods such as storyboarding and scenario building, persona building, rapid prototyping and parallel prototyping, cultural probes, academic research and evidence based design as well as hands on physical modelling and sketching.

Beyond the realms of physical interactions and CAD, I also enjoy graphic design and illustration. Although I have not been formally taught, I have picked up some tips and techniques throughout my time and consider myself to be fairly proficient. I mainly produce logo and icon designs for websites. I am also experienced in typography.

As a side interest and major hobby, I'm also experienced in web design. I have had around 6 months experience using HTML + CSS (LESS) and jQuery. I have used a number of Bootstrap applications as well as jQuery plugins such as fullpage.js and pagepiling.js to create website prototypes. I built this website myself and it is iteration number 8!
