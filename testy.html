Micro Charge on the go

This project was completed in cooperation with Micro Mobility and Victoria University of Wellington. The purpose of the project was to design & complimentary charging service for Micro customers. The service aims to provide Micro customers with an appealing and convenient method of charging electric scooters in order to encourage their widespread use. By doing so, Micro Charge on the Go builds on Micro Mobility's "world first" branding value to reinforce the idea that electric scooters are a useful and convenient urban transport option. With the ability to leave your scooter unattended as it charges, it is important to ensure that it is securely locked up until the owner is ready to retrieve it. For this reason, the bench has been designed to use a locking system to keep the scooter safely contained for a set duration of time. The system is comprised of several locking doorways which each lead to a cavity beneath the bench in which the scooter can be securely housed.

The Cradle - Binaural Audio Prototype

This project was completed in cooperation with Jacob Marsh, Caela Heke and The Comfort Group and Victoria University of Wellington. The purpose of the project was to create a tangible interaction around sleeping. The concept was based around the effects of binaural audio stimulation and its efficacy for encouraging relaxation.
